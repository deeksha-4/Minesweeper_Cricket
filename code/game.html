INSTRUCTIONS


SINGLE PLAYER

You can either use our classic field or create your own custom one.
Click on any square on the playing field to uncover it. Each click decreases the number of balls by one. If the square is empty, you score runs corresponding to the number on the square. However, if it contains a fielder ( ), the game ends and your score is final, even if you have more balls left. To win, you must use up all the balls without uncovering a fielder. Winning adds 6 runs to your final score!!

POWER-UPS

To spice things up a bit, there are power-ups (red) randomly distributed on the field

> Extra Life () : You can continue the game even if you uncover a fielder! But remember, it works only once.

> Minus Fielder () : One fielder is randomly removed from the field!

But wait! Not all "power-ups" are good! Watch out for the black ones, they could cost you dearly.

> Minus Two () : Your score goes down by 2 runs

> Plus Fielder () : An extra fielder is randomly added to the field

Once you've finished the game, you can save your score to the leaderboard! Good luck!!



TWO PLAYER - AGAINST

You can either use our classic field or create your own custom one.
In this mode, the players take turns setting the positions of fielders and power-ups on the field, and the other player uncovers squares. Whoever scores the highest wins!!


TWO PLAYER - RACE

You can either use our classic field or create your own custom one.
In this mode, both the players take turns choosing squares on the same field. At the end (when both players have encountered fielders), whoever has the highest score wins!!
